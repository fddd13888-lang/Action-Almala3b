ACTION ALMALA3B

Action Almala3b

Football Card Game

Objective

Build a complete formation of 11 players and win! 🏆

Win Conditions — All 3 Required

Condition 01
Valid Formation
4-3-3 4-4-2 5-3-2
Condition 02
At least one Captain ⭐C
Condition 03
A star in each line
A star ⭐ in Defense, Midfield & Attack — or consecutive numbers in any line:
Defense: 2-3-4 or 3-4-5  |  Midfield: 6-7-8  |  Attack: 9-10-11

How to Play

  • Each player starts with 5 cards
  • Each turn: draw 2 cards, then play up to 2 cards — or Skip
  • Hand limit: max 7 cards
  • Field limit: max 11 players
