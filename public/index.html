🎮 Action Almala3b

A simple and fun football card game

🏆 Objective

Build a formation of 11 players

✅ Win Conditions (All 3 required)

1️⃣ Valid Formation:
4-3-3
4-4-2
5-3-2
2️⃣ At least one Captain (⭐C)
3️⃣ A star in each line (Defense, Midfield & Attack)
OR 3 consecutive numbers in any line:

Defense: 2-3-4 or 3-4-5
Midfield: 6-7-8
Attack: 9-10-11

📌 How to Play

Each player starts with 5 cards

Each turn:

  • Draw 2 cards
  • Play up to 2 cards
  • Or Skip

⚠️ Limits:

  • Hand cards: max 7 cards
  • Field cards: max 11 players

🃏 Regular Cards

These are the cards you use to build your formation:

🧤 Goalkeeper
🛡️ Defender
🎯 Midfielder
⚽ Forward

Some have a star ⭐ or captain ⭐C

💥 Special Cards

🃏 Joker
Can be placed in any position — all are stars ⭐
Counts as any missing number in a consecutive sequence
✍️ Contract
Permanently steal a player from your opponent
🤝 Loan
Borrow a player from your opponent for 2 turns only
They return automatically after
🔄 Swap
Exchange one of your players with an opponent's
Must be the same position
🔴 Red Card
Permanently remove a player from your opponent's field
🟡 Yellow Card
Warning to a player
2 warnings = automatic expulsion
🚫 Offside
Opponent skips their next turn
🗑️ Trash Player
Remove a player from your own formation
Useful when you have a wrong formation (e.g. 4-5-1)
and want to fix it (e.g. 4-4-2)
❌ Cancel Order
Cancels any special card played against you
